# 015-Lung-Cancer-Analysis-SQL-Project

🎗️ Cancer Patient Analytics Using SQL
📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates SQL-based exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a cancer patient dataset. The objective is to analyze patient demographics, cancer progression, treatment outcomes, survival rates, and health risk factors to uncover meaningful insights that support healthcare research and data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze patient demographic characteristics.
Understand the distribution of cancer stages at diagnosis.
Evaluate lifestyle and health risk factors.
Measure treatment effectiveness and survival outcomes.
Compare patient survival across treatments and countries.
Analyze diagnosis and treatment trends over time.
Identify relationships between chronic conditions and survival.
Demonstrate SQL techniques for healthcare analytics.

📊 Business Questions Solved

👥 Patient Demographics Analysis

1. Average patient age
Calculates the average age of all patients.

2. Gender distribution
Measures the number of male and female patients.

3. Country with the highest number of cases
Identifies where the largest patient population originates.

4. Distribution of cancer stages
Analyzes how patients are distributed across different stages of cancer at diagnosis.

❤️ Health & Risk Factor Analysis

5. Patients with a family history of cancer
Determines how many patients have hereditary cancer risk.

6. Average BMI by cancer stage
Examines whether BMI varies across cancer stages.

7. Smoking status and cancer stage
Analyzes the relationship between smoking and cancer progression.

8. Patients with chronic conditions
Measures the prevalence of additional medical conditions such as hypertension, asthma, cirrhosis, and other cancers.

9. Cholesterol levels and survival rates
Compares patient survival rates across different cholesterol categories.

💊 Treatment & Outcome Analysis

10. Most common treatment by cancer stage
Identifies treatment approaches commonly used at each stage.

11. Average treatment duration
Calculates the average number of days between diagnosis and treatment completion.

12. Survival rate by treatment type
Evaluates treatment effectiveness based on patient survival.

13. Country with the highest survival rate
Compares healthcare outcomes across countries.

14. Survival rate based on family history
Determines whether hereditary factors influence survival outcomes.

📅 Time-Based Analysis

15. Year with the highest number of diagnoses
Identifies peak diagnosis years.

16. Survival trends by treatment completion year
Examines treatment completion activity across different years.

17. Median survival time by cancer stage
Uses CTEs and Window Functions to calculate median treatment duration for each cancer stage.

📈 Comparative & Pattern Analysis

18. BMI comparison between survivors and non-survivors
Determines whether BMI differs significantly based on survival status.

19. Smoking status and survival rates
Compares survival outcomes between smokers and non-smokers.

20. Most common chronic conditions among survivors
Analyzes which chronic illnesses are most frequently found among surviving patients.

21. Age group versus cancer stage
Evaluates whether younger patients are diagnosed at earlier cancer stages.

22. Cancer stage with the longest treatment duration
Measures average treatment duration across cancer stages.

⭐ Key Takeaways

This project demonstrates how SQL can transform healthcare data into actionable insights for clinical research and healthcare management. Through demographic analysis, treatment evaluation, survival analysis, and advanced SQL techniques such as CTEs, window functions, and conditional aggregation, it showcases practical skills in healthcare analytics and business intelligence. The project is well-suited for demonstrating proficiency in SQL for Healthcare Data Analyst, Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, Clinical Research Analyst, and SQL Developer roles.
